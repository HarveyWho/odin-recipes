Dongpo Meat

Descriptions

Dongpo Meat, also known as braised pork belly, is a traditional Chinese dish dating to thousands of years ago. According to legend, this dish is invented by the great poet Su Dongpo from Song Dynasty. Under about 3 hours of slow cooking in a pot, the meat is braised very tender and full of scent.

Ingredients

  • Green onion
  • Pork belly
  • Ginger
  • Soy sauce
  • Ice sugar

Steps

  1. Lay ginger on bottom of the pot
  2. Put cleaned pork belly on top of it
  3. Add soy sauce and sugar
  4. Heat the pot for about 3 hours
  5. Before turning off the heat, add green onions on top and cook for 5 mins
  6. Slice the whole meat into cubes and serve it
